Feed menu
=========

Sign In...
   Sign into your TrueFX account.
Sign Out
   Sign out of your TrueFX account, and clear the saved password.
Symbols...
   Customize the symbols available by with WebQuery, and displayed in the Dealing Rates table.
Pause
   Stop updating from the data feed.
Resume
   Use Resume if your computer has been sleeping, or you manually paused the data feed.
Reload
   Reload the current market data.
Recalculate
   Recalculate the Pip Cost.
Options (or Preferences)
   Show the Preferences dialog.
Exit (or Quit)
   Close all Benchmark windows and quit.
